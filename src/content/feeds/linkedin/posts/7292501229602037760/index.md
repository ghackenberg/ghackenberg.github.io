Impressions from over three years of CADdrive 𝐝𝐞𝐯𝐞𝐥𝐨𝐩𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐭 and three semesters of CADdrive 𝐮𝐬𝐚𝐠𝐞 in product and engineering design education at FH OÖ Campus Wels | School of Engineering. Also, last year we made three major features accessible to the public:

1️⃣ A proper visualization of the #CAD model revision graph based on graph traversal algorithms for computing the graph layout and scalable vector graphs (#SVG) for rendering the graph.
2️⃣ Native support for FreeCAD models as well as the STandard for the Exchange of Product Model Data (#STEP) based on OpenCASCADE Technology (#OCCT) from Capgemini.
3️⃣ An integrated editor for #LDraw (i.e. digital #LEGO) models with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface and automatic tracking of change operations (i.e. add, select, move, remove bricks).
