📢 We have just released a new version of CADdrive supporting #FreeCAD models out-of-the-box! Our students will use this feature in the coming four months in our project-based course on 𝐀𝐠𝐢𝐥𝐞 𝐏𝐫𝐨𝐝𝐮𝐜𝐭 𝐃𝐞𝐬𝐢𝐠𝐧.

𝘍𝘰𝘳 𝘵𝘩𝘰𝘴𝘦, 𝘸𝘩𝘰 𝘢𝘳𝘦 𝘪𝘯𝘵𝘦𝘳𝘦𝘴𝘵𝘦𝘥 𝘪𝘯 𝘵𝘦𝘤𝘩𝘯𝘪𝘤𝘢𝘭 𝘥𝘦𝘵𝘢𝘪𝘭𝘴: The implementation is based on #Zip.js for unzipping the FreeCAD file contents as well as #OpenCascade.js for #BRep parsing and meshing. The BRep parsing and meshing is outsourced to a #WebWorker for performance reasons. And finally, for rendering we automatically translate the prepared BReps into binary #GLTF models.

Try yourself at CADdrive.com or visit our community website CADdrive.org!

Note that due to the integration of OpenCascade.js, we are only one step away from supporting #STEP models. Probably we will implement and release this feature in the coming few weeks.
