Today, we completed our first CADdrive course at 𝐅𝐇 𝐎𝐎𝐄 𝐒𝐜𝐡𝐨𝐨𝐥 𝐨𝐟 𝐄𝐧𝐠𝐢𝐧𝐞𝐞𝐫𝐢𝐧𝐠. Over the past semester, eleven 𝐈𝐧𝐧𝐨𝐯𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 𝐚𝐧𝐝 𝐏𝐫𝐨𝐝𝐮𝐜𝐭 𝐌𝐚𝐧𝐚𝐠𝐞𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐭 (𝐈𝐏𝐌) 𝐌𝐚𝐬𝐭𝐞𝐫 students worked on an optimized #Glasshouse design including requirements engineering, architectural design, as well as structural, thermal, mechanical and electrical simulation. The results are quite impressive, I believe! ❤

Here is some technical information about our 𝐬𝐨𝐟𝐭𝐰𝐚𝐫𝐞 𝐬𝐭𝐚𝐜𝐤: For #CAD / virtual #LEGO modeling we relied on #LeoCAD and the amazing #LDraw data format and parts library. For structural and thermal simulation we used SimScale and Code Aster as well as #OpenFOAM (see The OpenFOAM Foundation). Finally, for mechanical and electrical simulation we used #OpenModelica (see the Modelica Association).

Thank you Ana Cristina Avila Brizuela, Aso Ayatonabi, Rümeysa Çalışkan, Yazeed Haddadin, Mohsen Karami, Genaro Hernan Ramirez Escalante, Mehdi S., Maria Dolores Santillan Pichardo, Barbara Strauss, Luis Guillermo Torres Pico, and Denizay Zerik for your participation and the effort, you spent on the course and this project!

The course has been co-supervised by Christian Zehetner (Professor for Product Development) and Georg Hackenberg (Professor for Industrial Informatics).
