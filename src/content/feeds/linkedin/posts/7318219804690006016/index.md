Just finished my talk "On modeling, discrete-event simulation, and optimization of spine layouts" at CIRP Conference on Manufacturing Systems 2025 (CMS'25), University of Twente, Netherlands organized by Eric Lutters and team. It is very inspiring to be at this leading international conference and learn about cutting edge R&D in the manufacturing industry! 👍

Also, thank you very much for the very helpful discussion on the control optimization problem (i.e. job routing and scheduling) and possible solutions. The idea of using a weighted product type / operation type / machine instance graph with updating weights for solving the optimization problems seems very promising, so we might explore this next! 📈

Thank you also Chiara Sammarco and Antonio Padovano for the fruitful collaboration on this research project! Chiara started this research project under my guidance during her Master thesis at FH OÖ Campus Wels | School of Engineering as part of our joint Master program "Innovation, Product, and Engineering Management" with University of Calabria, Italy. 🎓

Also thank you Sri Kolla 🟥 for taking the picture and hosting our session on "Logistics and system design"!
