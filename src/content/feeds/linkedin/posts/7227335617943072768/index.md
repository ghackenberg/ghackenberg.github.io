I am happy to share, that CADdrive now supports #FBX, #COLLADA, #GLTF, #PLY, and #STL models through the amazing THREE.js loader components. 𝐓𝐡𝐢𝐬 𝐜𝐡𝐚𝐧𝐠𝐞 𝐠𝐫𝐞𝐚𝐭𝐥𝐲 𝐞𝐧𝐜𝐡𝐚𝐧𝐜𝐞𝐬 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐚𝐩𝐩𝐥𝐢𝐜𝐚𝐛𝐢𝐥𝐢𝐭𝐲 𝐨𝐟 𝐂𝐀𝐃𝐝𝐫𝐢𝐯𝐞 𝐢𝐧 𝐝𝐢𝐟𝐟𝐞𝐫𝐞𝐧𝐭 𝐝𝐨𝐦𝐚𝐢𝐧𝐬!

However, note that we support 𝐭𝐞𝐱𝐭𝐮𝐫𝐞𝐬 𝐨𝐧𝐥𝐲 𝐨𝐧 𝐛𝐢𝐧𝐚𝐫𝐲 𝐆𝐋𝐓𝐅 𝐦𝐨𝐝𝐞𝐥𝐬 at the moment. We actively work on texture support for the other model formats as well. I will let you know as soon as we have solved the remaining issues here.
