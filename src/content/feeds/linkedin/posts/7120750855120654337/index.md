CADdrive now has a new email notification feature (based on #Nodemailer and #MJML) 👨‍💻 We included the feature to facilitate #asynchronous #collaboration between the team members of our Master-level #product #design course at FH OÖ Campus Wels | School of Engineering.

We had this idea because of #testing with our students! While watching and listening to them, we noticed that they will work in #distributed locations and asynchronously. So we need an #effective means to spread the latest information among the team members.

#TestingInTheWild simply is the best strategy for driving #agile software projects! You should not fear the #feedback of your users, but you should absolutely embrace it! Only then you will be able to leave the #ivory #tower of your software lab 😉

Is our software ready yet for productive use at other academic and research institutions? We will find out 🙂
