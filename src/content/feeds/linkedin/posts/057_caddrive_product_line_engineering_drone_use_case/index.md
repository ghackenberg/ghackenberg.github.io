We started working on CADdrive for studying (and teaching) product line engineering. Our first use case is the drone product line shown below. We took the idea for that product line from a paper on the same use case, which concentrates on the corresponding feature tree.

The tree describes three drone configurations: A basic and cheap configuration, a medium configuration with better cameras, and a large configuration with the biggest battery and other auxiliary equipment.

Now we use these models to prepare a click prototype for the new CADdrive product line engineering extension. With this extension we want to improve product line engineering skills and training programmes.

Feel free to get in touch, if you would like to support our mission!
