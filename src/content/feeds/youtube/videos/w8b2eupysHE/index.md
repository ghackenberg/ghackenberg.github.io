description: "In this video we show our modeling and simulation technique for spine layouts in action. Spine layouts comprise a main corridor (red color) and a variable number of side corridors (green color) with robots for material transport. Furthermore, on the side corridors machines are located performing certain manufacturing and assembly operations.\n\nOur technique allows factory planners to explore the spine layout design space quickly and effectively (i.e. different number of corridors and machines per corridor). Also, our approach provides the foundation for discrete-event simulation and performance evaluation of system designs. During system operation, our technique can be used for deriving optimal job routing and scheduling strategies and optimizing machine utilization and system throughput.\n\nEnjoy!"
