### Research-Backed Strategy and Engineering Integrity

Navigating modern technology landscapes requires a bridge between academic research, engineering execution, and high-level corporate strategy.

Leveraging my PhD background, active university research, and past tenure as an IoT Business Unit Manager, I provide independent, research-backed advisory to tech startups, enterprises, and venture funds. I evaluate software architectures, conduct technical due diligence, and advise on strategic roadmaps for emerging technologies.

#### How we can work together:
*   **Investor & Venture Capital Due Diligence:** Authoritative technology audits, code quality evaluations, and scalability assessments of deep-tech startups before funding rounds or acquisitions.
*   **Enterprise Architecture & Tech Roadmaps:** Designing future-proof strategies for migrating to modern web stacks, deploying cloud/edge IoT architectures, or integrating semantic database systems.
*   **Emerging Tech Feasibility Studies:** Helping organizations evaluate the risks and opportunities of local Large Language Models (LLMs), simulation-based optimization, and intelligent agent systems.
