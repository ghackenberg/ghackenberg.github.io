<p>
			The following screenshot shows the zumida dashboard after login.
			The dashboard consists of six parts:
		</p>
		<ol>
			<li>The <strong>list</strong> of active zumida topics grouped by last modification date (i.e. today, yesterday, this week, this month, etc.).</li>
			<li>A <strong>text input</strong> for searching the available zumida topics or creating a new zumida topic (when pressing the <code>Enter</code> key).</li>
			<li>The <strong>number of contents</strong> inside each of the zumida topics scaled with respect to the maximum number of contents per topic in each group and tinged with the topic color.</li>
			<li>The <strong>number of cross-references</strong> within and between zumida topics scaled with respect to the maximum number of cross-references per topic in each group.</li>
			<li>The <strong>name</strong> of each zumida topic.</li>
			<li>A <strong>button</strong> for archiving active zumida topics.</li>
		</ol>
		<p>
			When clicking or tapping the name of a zumida topic you enter the explorer view explained in the <a href="/posts/2016_09_28_zumida_user_interface/">previous post</a>.
		</p>

			</a>
		</p>
