The video shows a control strategy for 25 households including their refrigerators.
The strategy is selected according to a weather scenario, in particular considering available sun energy.
The visualization uses WebGL and SceneJS for 3D rendering and Google Charts for the diagrams.

<iframe title="Video player" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/bpUekKVIfds?feature=player_detailpage" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="yes"></iframe>

In the future we plan to extend our tool chain and develop a more realistic model of **EnergyCity**.
The testbed also will be used in a practical course at university to teach students about the smart grid, model-driven engineering and distributed control.
As soon as we have a stable version ready we plan to release the models and tools for others.
