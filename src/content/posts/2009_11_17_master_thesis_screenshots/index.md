<p>
			I have prepared two screenshots for you, both showing the live view of my <em>visual workbench</em> application.
			The live view directly processes the video stream delivered by the USB camera, calculates the hand position and draws the results.
			I use <a class="web" href="http://www.wxwidgets.org" target="_blank">wxWidgets</a> and <a class="web" href="http://opencv.willowgarage.com/" target="_blank">OpenCV</a> as third-party, open source dependencies.
		</p>
